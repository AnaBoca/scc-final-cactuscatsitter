Services

Below are the Cactus Cat Sitter service package listings. Each contracted visit will include services performed by one of our trained sitters. All sitters at Cactus Cat Sitter travel to the cat owner's home to take care of the cats. We perform a comprehensive intake visit over the phone prior to a contract. Among gathering information about your cat's in-home pet supplies, such as food and medications, we make sure to intake all emergency protocol information, such as the cat's preferred vet and emergency clinic.

*In the event that the emergency protocol needs to be enacted, our trained professionals will escort your cat to the agreed-upon facility.

Take a look below for more on our individual service package offerings.

  • Silver Standard

  • Premium Double

  • Comprehensive Gold

$20.00 USD per day (2-cat limit)

The Silver Standard plan is our most popular service package and includes:

  • Free-feed 1X per day
  • Ten minute play session 1X per day
  • Litter scoop 1X per day
  • Fur comb 1X per day
  • Eye booger clean-up 1X per day
  • *Full litter box clean-out every 7 days for 7-day contract or longer

$40.00 USD per day (2-cat limit)

The Premium Double plan moves some care services of the Silver Standard plan to two daily visits (separated by 4-hour intervals) and includes:

  • Feeding 2X per day
  • Ten minute play session 2X per day
  • Litter scoop 1X per day
  • Fur comb 1X per day
  • Eye booger clean-up 2X per day
  • *Full litter box clean-out every 7 days for 7-day contract or longer

$60.00 USD per day (2-cat limit)

The Comprehensive Gold package has three daily visits (separated by 4 hours intervals) and includes:

  • Feeding 3X per day
  • Ten minute play session 2X per day
  • Litter scoop 1X per day
  • Fur comb 1X per day
  • Eye booger clean-up 2X per day
  • Full-room vacuum in primary cat space 1X per day
  • *Full litter box clean-out every 7 days for 7-day contract or longer
